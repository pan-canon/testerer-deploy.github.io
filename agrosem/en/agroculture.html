Biological pesticides

There is a global trend towards reducing the use of chemical plant protection products. Eco-friendly methods of dealing with pests, diseases, and weeds are becoming more popular. Microbes used for biological protection can make plants healthy by controlling pest and disease populations. Their preventive use is also possible.

Biological stimulants

Biological stimulants are usually obtained from plant or animal raw materials. Such preparations are quite effective, as they are low molecular weight organic compounds. Beneficial bacteria, fungi, carbohydrates, amino acids, algae, mycorrhizae are most often used for their production. The use of such preparations helps to increase the yield and preserve the quality of the soil.

Biological inoculants

To realize the yield potential, the plant consumes many nutrients, in particular, nutrients that come to it both from the soil and through the leaves. The most valuable nutrient for plants, the "element of life" is nitrogen. Green plants consume only the mineral form of nitrogen, which is very scarce in the soil. Therefore, to supply nitrogen, it is necessary to always replenish nitrogen in the soil with mineral nitrogen fertilizers. However, plants such as legumes are able to provide themselves with mineral nitrogen in a natural way - due to symbiosis with nitrogen-fixing bacteria from the genus Rhizobium.
